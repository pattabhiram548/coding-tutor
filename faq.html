Frequently Asked Questions

What is Coding Tutor™?

Coding Tutor™ is a program by AJA designed to help individuals gain practical coding skills and become industry ready.

Why are skills more important than knowledge in IT?

Skills involve practical abilities to perform tasks, while knowledge is just information. IT companies seek skilled individuals who can apply their knowledge effectively.

Who can join the Coding Tutor™ program?

Any graduate with logical thinking, basic communication skills, and classroom training in Full Stack Development in Java, Dot Net, Python, or DevOps can join.

What will I learn in Coding Tutor™?

You will learn coding skills through exercises, assignments, and projects. Additionally, you'll receive training in communication and corporate skills.

How long is the Coding Tutor™ program?

The program lasts for 6 months, with 1200 hours of skills improvement overall, which includes 1000 hours of coding practice.

Is the Coding Tutor™ program online or in-person?

The program is in-person and requires you to attend the office regularly.

What technologies are covered in Coding Tutor™?

Currently, the program covers Java/J2EE Full Stack Development, Dot Net Full Stack Development, Python Full Stack Development, and DevOps Engineering.

How many days a week do I need to attend?

The program runs 6 days a week. (Monday to Saturday)

Are there any prerequisites for joining the Coding Tutor™ program?

Yes, you need to have completed basic training in the respective technology.

Is work from home available in the Coding Tutor™ program?

No, the program requires you to be present in the office for focused learning.

What is the cost of the Coding Tutor™ program?

The investment for the program is 3.69 L inclusive of taxes.

Is there a stipend during the internship?

Yes, a monthly stipend of 10,000 INR will be paid during the 6-month internship.

Do I need to bring my own laptop?

Yes, AJA follows a Bring Your Own Device (BYOD) policy for the internship.

What do I get after completing the internship?

You will receive an Internship Certificate, Relieving and Experience Certificate, 6 months of stipend payslips, Form 16, and unlock the job offer as a Full Stack Developer at AJA.

Is there a job guarantee after the internship?

Yes, AJA offers a job offer as a Full Stack Developer with a starting CTC of 3.6 Lacs per annum on day 1 of your internship, and this gets unlocked on completion of the 6 months internship.

What will be my designation during the program?

During the internship, your designation will be Trainee Software Engineer. Upon successful completion of the program, you will be promoted to Software Engineer.

Is relocation mandatory?

Depending on the project, relocation may be applicable. You may need to relocate as per project requirements.

Are there employee benefits during the internship?

Employee benefits like Group Medical Insurance, ESI, and PF are applicable once you join AJA as a full-time employee after the internship.

Where is the work location?

You will initially be based in Hyderabad but may be deployed to other locations across India.

Is there any bond with AJA?

No, there is no bond. However, a 3-month notice period is required if you are working on a customer project.

How are Coding Tutors selected?

Coding Tutors are selected through a stringent process, including verifying their experience and evaluating their coding skills through a demo.

Are Coding Tutors paid?

Yes, Coding Tutors are paid between $10 to $25 per hour based on their experience and skills.

You will also learn communication skills and corporate skills necessary for the IT industry.

Why is this program considered an investment?

The program is considered an investment because it aims to provide long-term career growth and skills that generate future opportunities.

The 1000 hours are divided into three stages: Basic/Fundamental Skills (350 hours), Intermediate Skills (500 hours), and Advanced Skills (150 hours).

You will work on real-world exercises, assignments, Proof of Concepts (PoCs), and a comprehensive project in your technology stream.
