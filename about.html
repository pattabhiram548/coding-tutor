About the CodingTutor

Explore the realms of innovation and technology with Aja, where we merge creativity and expertise to redefine possibilities in the digital landscape.
Welcome to Aja Coding Tutor LLP, a beacon of opportunity in the IT industry, founded in 2022 by Mr. Phaniraj Jaligama. Phaniraj, a distinguished entrepreneur and seasoned leader, brings over 25 years of experience from diversified industries including Government, Administration, IT services, IT products, and IT captives. Having served in pivotal roles at TCS, Cognizant, and TIBCO Software, he has a proven track record of driving success. Notably, as CEO, he has previously built an IT services company from a team of two to a thriving workforce of over 700 professionals. His journey is marked by a passion for mentorship and motivational speaking, inspiring countless individuals along the way. Aja Coding Tutor LLP was established with a clear vision: to bridge the gap between companies and aspiring professionals. Phaniraj believes that everyone has untapped potential, and with the right opportunities, anyone can secure a fulfilling career. This philosophy is at the core of everything we do at Aja Coding Tutor.
